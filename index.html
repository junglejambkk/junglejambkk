THE UNDERGROUND

We are Bangkok's premier underground bass music collective, dedicated to bringing the authentic rave experience to Thailand's capital. From intimate warehouse gatherings to massive underground events, we create spaces where the bass hits different and the community comes together.

Our mission is simple: keep the underground alive. We champion the raw, unfiltered energy of drum & bass, jungle, and hardcore electronic music, creating events that honor the culture while pushing boundaries.
